The Block: The Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps (IRGC) has NOT consented. Iran's hard-line security establishment prioritizes denying the U.S./Israel victory over ending the war.

Twin Narratives: "Trump Says Iran Deal Is Close" vs "Tehran Says No Decision Made" — classic information war. Both are true from their respective vantage points.

Oil Impact: Crude -3.73% to $84.44. Markets already pricing some peace premium.
