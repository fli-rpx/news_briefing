Iran Fires Missiles at Israel; Regional War Erupts

Trump called Israeli PM Netanyahu to urge restraint — reportedly telling him he would be "in prison without US support." The Pentagon raised Israel's counter-intelligence threat to "critical" after discovering listening devices in Israeli facilities.

Hormuz Blockade: 5 Months and Counting

Iran effectively controls the Strait of Hormuz since February 28, cutting off 20% of global oil supply. OPEC output hit 35-year lows. The Pentagon's $29B war spending includes $8B on naval operations to reopen the waterway — with no success.

百年未有之大变局 · 金式幽默

霍尔木兹海峡，宽仅33公里，承载全球约五分之一的能源供应。这条窄水道不仅是石油的通道，更是美元环流的命门——全球石油以美元计价、以美元结算，而霍尔木兹就是这条货币大动脉上最脆弱的一环。
